Spaghetti Aglio Olio

a quick and easy dish

It's been a week since you've been to the grocery store and your stomach is as empty as your fridge? Don't order food just yet - with only a few basic ingredients you can make yourself a real italian dish; Spaghetti Aglio Olio

Ingredients

  • salt
  • any spaghetti
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • parmigiano (optional)
  • fresh basil (optional)

start cooking

  1. boil the spaghetti in salty water as long as necessary
  2. heat the olive oil in a pan on medium heat
  3. cut the garlic in small pieces, or press it and add it into the warm/almost hot olive oil. put it aside for at least 3 minutes and let taste transfer.
  4. add the spaghetti (and a bit of the water you cooked them in) into the pan and stir well, add salt if necessary
  5. (optional) add parmigiano and fresh basil
  6. enjoy your meal
